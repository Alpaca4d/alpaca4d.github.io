Alpaca4d is a Grasshopper plugin which has been developed on top of OpenSees. The library is mostly used by researchers and academia because of the not userfriendly interface even if the math behind the library is highly sophisticated. The main idea of Alpaca4d is to provide an efficient and easy way to use OpenSees without writing any line of code. The belief is to bring more users to perform structural analyses with Opensees in a parametric environment such as Grasshopper. Our main fields of interests are structural analysis, optimization, machine learning, geometry manipulation.

Alpaca4d is free and always will be. Feel free to offer us a coffee with a small donation.
